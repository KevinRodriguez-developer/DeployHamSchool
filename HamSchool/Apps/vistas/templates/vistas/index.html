About Ham-School

The best english option

This project consists of teaching interactive English courses to primary and secondary school children through a digital medium, with the purpose of helping their learning and orienting them to better learning.

About us

Ham-School is a small company of 4 members with a commercial twist, in charge of the development of the Ham-School website focused on learning the English language. Ham-School provides basic knowledge at the preschool-secondary level to expand or learn a new language that would be English. Our website will have educational games and images that will facilitate children's understanding of the language.

Creation of Ham-School

Ham-School was created in September 2020 to provide basic knowledge of the English language, an idea proposed by the project manager, our team keeps an eye on the needs that arise to provide a quality website and good learning.

Ham-School is located in Parque Industrial Las Americas, Parque industrial El Saucito, Chihuahua Local Periférico # 504
